Sample menu

Built around your event

What's below is representative, not exhaustive. Every booking gets a tasting list put together around guest count, event type and slot.

Starters & BBQ

Seekh kebab, chicken tikka, reshmi kebab, fish fry, spring rolls.

Soups

Hot & sour, chicken corn — a choice of two per event.

Main course

Chicken, mutton and beef karahi, chicken handi, daal — across day and night slots.

Chicken or mutton biryani, vegetable pulao, naan, roghni naan.

Desserts & beverages

Kheer, gulab jamun, soft drinks, mineral water, tea and coffee.
