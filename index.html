The venue

Six hundred guests, or sixty

The venue partitions, so a hundred-guest engagement doesn't sit inside a space built for six hundred. Four decorated stages to choose from, table service and mineral water included, and sound, entry setups and female waitresses available on request.

Standby generator, covered parking, separate washrooms, a bridal room and air cooling throughout.
