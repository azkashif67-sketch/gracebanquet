About Us

Thirty-two years at the same venue.

Since 1994

Co-pioneers of the business

We opened in Korangi in 1994, when few others had. Thirty-two years later the venue is still ours and the standard hasn't moved.

More than five thousand events have run through this space — weddings, birthdays, engagements, iftari, corporate evenings. Every one treated like the first.

Unbeatable rates and an experience worth remembering. That was the promise in 1994 and it hasn't needed rewriting.
